<p><p>Idle gears are used in mechanical systems to manage the motion of rotating shafts without transferring power. In a single idle gear market, a single gear is employed to change direction or isolate motion between two gears, commonly found in basic transmissions. Both types are essential in various applications, including automotive and industrial mechanisms.</p></p>
